INSTRUCTION-CUM-GUIDELINES FOR VERIFICATION OF DOCUMENTS

The HP. Board has developed as Online Application to apply for Verification of academic documents and certificates from this Board. The candidetes registered with this Board and any other ageny can verify the certificates of those candidates who are registered with this Board though the online application by uploading the relevent particulars of the candidate/candidates.

The Board shall charge a fee of Rs. 600/- each or as prescribed by it from time to time for verification of various certificates issed by the Board,which shell be paied online through Gateway , Offline fee will not be excepted in any case. Online link for payment is available with application.

Compulsory Information:-

1. Click Proceed to Certificate Verification button displayed below.
2. Agency Login/Registrar For Student Verification Page is opened.
3. If you want to Verify the Student Details, Click on New Registration Link button.
4. Now new Registration Page will open.
5. Enter the complete detail as per mention in the Labels in the page like Agency/Office/Deptt. Name, Agency/Office/Deptt. Address, Agency/Office/Deptt, Pin Code, Agency Mob-No, Agency Email ID, User Name, Password. After entering these Details, click on Submit button.
6. After that go to Already Registered/Log In Link Button
7. Now Agency Login page is opened. Enter Username and Password.
8. Click on ‘Add Student Details’ and enter the required Details of the Student as written below.
9. Enter Student Name.
10. Enter Student Mobile No & Email address.
11. Enter Student Roll Number
12. Select Examination Type from Selection drop down box.
13. Select Academic Year from Selection drop down box.
14. Select Academic Session from Selection drop down box.
15. Select Class from Selection drop down box.
16. Click on Submit Detail Button.
17. Uncheck Add Student Detail for Displaying the Details of Students entered in the System.
18. Click on ‘Pay Total Fee Amount’ for paying the Verification Fees.
